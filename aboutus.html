About Us

Nestled in the heart of Surat—India’s diamond capital—we are a passionate team of skilled artisans, gemologists, and visionaries with a legacy rooted in craftsmanship and innovation. Surat is known for cutting and polishing over 90% of the world’s diamonds, and we proudly contribute to that legacy with dedication and precision.

✦ Our Mission
To deliver exceptional quality diamonds and fine jewellery that combine tradition, technology, and trust. We aim to create timeless pieces that reflect beauty, elegance, and responsibility.

✦ What We Do?
From sourcing natural, conflict-free diamonds to precision cutting and exquisite finishing, every step is handled with care and expertise. Using state-of-the-art tools and time-honored techniques, we transform rough stones into radiant masterpieces.

✦ Our Promise
100% genuine, certified diamonds Ethical and transparent sourcing practices Skilled local craftsmanship Personalized customer service Global shipping and secure packaging Free returns/exchange on select items

✦ Why Surat?
Surat is not just our location—it's our identity. As the global hub of diamond polishing and trading, the city fuels our growth with unmatched talent, infrastructure, and community spirit. Our team includes expert karigars (craftsmen) who have decades of experience in turning raw brilliance into lasting beauty.

✦ Our Global Reach While our roots are firmly planted in India, our diamonds shine in homes across the world—from North America to Europe, the Middle East to Southeast Asia. We serve both individual buyers and wholesale clients, ensuring quality, trust, and timely delivery in every transaction.
